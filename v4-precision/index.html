FAQ

What is an EMC evaluation+
An Emergency Medical Condition determination is a documented finding by a qualified provider. Under Florida PIP law it unlocks the full $10,000 in benefits instead of the $2,500 default cap.
How fast can a patient be seen+
Most patients are seen the same day they reach out, seven days a week. Everything is telehealth, so there is no travel and no waiting room.
Is the 14-day deadline strict+
Florida PIP requires that initial care begin within 14 days of the accident. Same-day telehealth is the most reliable way to document a compliant determination inside the window.
How do attorneys refer+
Through the secure attorney referral portal. Submit a client in about two minutes, track status, and receive the signed report directly.
How are reports delivered+
Signed determinations are delivered securely, typically within 48 hours, formatted and ready for the patient file or demand package.
Is information kept private+
Yes. Intake, consent, and video visits run on HIPAA-conscious, encrypted systems, with strict safeguards for protected health information.
