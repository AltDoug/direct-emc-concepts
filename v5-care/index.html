The 14-day window

Care has to begin within 14 days. We make that simple: a caring provider, same day, from your couch, so you never lose the coverage you are owed.

Questions, answered gently

What is an EMC evaluation?
It is a documented finding by a licensed provider. Under Florida PIP law it is what keeps your full $10,000 in benefits available instead of the $2,500 default cap.
How soon can I be seen?
Most people are seen the same day they reach out, seven days a week. Because it is telehealth, there is no travel and no waiting room.
Do I really have only 14 days?
Florida PIP requires that your care begin within 14 days of the accident. A same-day telehealth visit is the easiest way to stay inside that window.
What if my attorney refers me?
Great. They can submit you through the secure referral portal in about two minutes, and we take it from there.
How will I get my report?
Your signed determination is delivered securely, typically within 48 hours, ready for your file or your attorney.
Is my information private?
Yes. Intake, consent, and your video visit run on HIPAA-conscious, encrypted systems, with strict safeguards for your health data.
