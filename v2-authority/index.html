The PIP clock

Every hour after the crash puts coverage at risk.

Florida law caps an accident patient at $2,500 in PIP benefits unless a qualified provider documents an Emergency Medical Condition. With that determination, the limit opens to the full $10,000.

Care must begin within 14 days. Miss it, and the coverage and the case value go with it. Direct EMC closes the gap with same-day, defensible evaluations.
