The PIP clock is ticking

Miss the EMC determination, and $7,500 in coverage disappears.

Under Florida's PIP statute, an accident patient is capped at $2,500 in benefits unless a qualified provider documents an Emergency Medical Condition. With a valid EMC determination, that limit opens to the full $10,000.

The catch: initial care must begin within 14 days of the accident, and many patients struggle to get a fast, properly documented evaluation. Delays cost patients coverage and cost your case its value.

Direct EMC exists to close that gap. We deliver compliant, same-day evaluations by telehealth so the determination is documented while it still counts.

Questions, answered

What is an EMC evaluation and why does it matter?
An Emergency Medical Condition (EMC) determination is a documented finding by a qualified provider. Under Florida PIP law, it is what unlocks the full $10,000 in benefits instead of the $2,500 default cap. Without it, patients and their cases are left with a fraction of the available coverage.
How fast can a patient be seen?
Most patients are seen the same day they reach out, seven days a week. Because everything is telehealth, there is no waiting room and no travel for an injured patient.
Is the 14-day deadline really that strict?
Florida PIP requires that initial medical care begin within 14 days of the accident. Same-day telehealth evaluations are the most reliable way to get a compliant determination documented well inside that window.
How do attorneys refer patients?
Through the secure attorney referral portal. Submit a client in about two minutes, track the evaluation status, and receive the signed report back directly. No phone tag, no fax machine.
How are reports delivered?
Signed EMC determinations are delivered securely, typically within 48 hours of the evaluation, formatted and ready to drop into the patient file or demand package.
Is my information kept private?
Yes. Intake, consent, and video visits run on HIPAA-conscious, encrypted systems, and protected health information is handled with strict safeguards at every step.
